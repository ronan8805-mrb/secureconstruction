About Secure Construction So Ltd

Your trusted partner for professional construction and installation services

Who We Are

Secure Construction So Ltd is a professional construction and specialist installation company based in Drogheda, Co. Louth. We deliver high-quality construction, installation, and finishing services for residential projects throughout the region.

Our team brings together expertise across multiple construction disciplines, from traditional building works to specialist fire protection services. We pride ourselves on our commitment to safety, quality, and full compliance with all building regulations and industry standards.

Our Experience

With extensive experience across diverse project types, Secure Construction So Ltd has built a strong reputation for delivering reliable, high-quality construction services. Our portfolio includes:

  • Residential Projects: Home extensions, renovations, new builds, and specialist installations

We understand the unique requirements of each project and adapt our approach to meet specific needs while maintaining our high standards across all works.

Local Focus

Based in Aston Village, Drogheda, we are proud to serve our local community and the wider Co. Louth area. Our local knowledge and established relationships with suppliers and trade professionals enable us to deliver projects efficiently while supporting the local economy.

Being locally based means we're always available for site visits, emergency works, and ongoing maintenance services. We're invested in the quality of our work because we're building in the community where we live and work.

Our Mission

To deliver safe, compliant, and high-quality construction and installation services that exceed client expectations and contribute to building a safer, better-built environment across Co. Louth.

We achieve this through:

  • Maintaining a safety-first approach on every site
  • Ensuring full compliance with all building regulations and standards
  • Using qualified, registered professionals for all specialist works
  • Delivering reliable workmanship and professional project coordination
  • Building long-term relationships based on trust and quality delivery

Commitment to Safety, Quality & Compliance

🛡️ Safety First

Every site operates under strict safety protocols. Full PPE compliance, risk assessments, and safety training are non-negotiable requirements for all our personnel and subcontractors.

✓ Quality Workmanship

We take pride in the quality of our work. From groundworks to finishing touches, every element is completed to the highest standards with attention to detail throughout.

📋 Full Compliance

All works are carried out in full compliance with building regulations, fire safety standards, and industry best practices. Specialist works are delivered by qualified, registered, or certified professionals.

🤝 Professional Coordination

We coordinate all aspects of your project, working seamlessly with architects, engineers, electricians, gas installers, and other trades to ensure correct installation and timely delivery.
